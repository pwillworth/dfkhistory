DFK History

DFK History is a 3rd party tool for use with Defi-Kingdoms, a cross-chain blockchain based Fantasy role playing game. The DFK History tool provides a detailed history of blockchain game events for heroes and pets. While this information is publicly available to all on the blockchain, it is for the most part encoded and hard to get to. It is presented here in an easily discoverable fashion.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does it take for exents to show up in history? Up to 2 hours.
  • How can I tell what user an address is for? If you click on an abbreviated address in any of the lists, the full address will be copied to your clipboard, and the DFK profile name of the address will be looked up and replace the address if available.
  • How do I know what stats went up for a listed level up? Hover over the summary in the Stat Ups column and a tool tip will pop up with the details.
  • What does the green bar mean on the pet display page? The bar represents how close to the maximum bonus value that pet has. You can hover over it to see the exact range values.
